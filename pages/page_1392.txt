报告题目: A geometric model of perfect derived categories of non-positive graded skew-gentle algebras
报告人：周宇 助理教授  （清华大学）
报告时间：2022年12月7日，10:00-11:00
报告地点： 腾讯会议ID 187-379-101                              
报告内容：
  For any non-positive graded skew-gentle algebra, there is an associated graded punctured marked surface, such that the indecomposable objects in its perfect derived category can be realized by the graded curves with local system on the surface. Moreover, the oriented intersections of two tagged graded curves give rise to a basis of the morphism space between the corresponding objects. This is a joint work with Yu Qiu and Chao Zhang
