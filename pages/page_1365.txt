报告题目: Inhomogeneous regularities and uniform boundedness of entropy to compressible Navier-Stokes in multi-dimensions
报告人：李进开教授（华南师范大学）
报告时间：2022年12月18日10：00-11：00
腾讯会议：584-598-996
报告内容：
   In this talk, we will consider the following two issues for the Cauchy problem to the heat conductive compressible Navier-Stokes equations in the presence of far field vacuum: (i) regularities of solutions in the inhomogeneous Sobolev spaces; (ii) uniform boundedness of the entropy. The previous works concerning these two issues focus on the case in one dimension. While in this talk, we will update the results for the case in multi-dimensions. This talk is based on a recent joint work with Prof . Zhouping Xin.
