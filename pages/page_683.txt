 一、学校介绍
 圣纳约基应用科技大学（SEAMK）位于芬兰西海岸的圣纳约基市，是芬兰国立应用科技大学，1992年合并三所学院成立.以其教育质量高、国际化程度高而闻名。是一所涵盖高等教学、基础研究、创新研发的综合型大学，名列芬兰应用科技大学排行榜第三。注重高质量教学与多元化实践的结合，全校有5000名全日制学生，其中10%为国际学生。教学人员380名。四个学院：商业及文化学院，农业及食品学院，健康管理及社会服务学院，技术学院，提供19个本科及8个硕士学位教学课程。其中3个学科用英语授课：国际工商管理本科，健康护理本科，MBA。技术学院和商业及文化学院为学生提供双学位证书。目前和超过50个国家的200所大学建立了合作关系。远景是成为一所具有全球化视野和企业家精神的大学。
 二、选拔要求
 1、2020级（2019级双学位5年毕业的）本科生, 学分平均绩点达3.2（有不及格成绩、违纪受过处分者不予报名）以上，身心健康，家庭同意并有能力资助国外生活学习
2、英语水平相当于B2水平，雅思6.0以上，TOEFL550分以上水平，若没有证书，需有其他有较强英语学习能力的证明或通过面试；
 圣纳约基应用科技大学要求专业需对应以下专业和英语开设课程
Seinäjoki University of Applied Sciences has four faculties.
 The School of Business and Culture offers education, research and development in the Frami campus in the fields of Business, Entrepreneurship, Cultural Management, Design and Library and Information Services.
 The School of Food and Agriculture offers education in Agriculture in Ilmajoki campus and the following in the Frami campus area in Seinäjoki: Hospitality Management, Food Processing and Biotechnology, and Forestry.
 The School of Health Care and Social Work consists of the unit of Heath Care in Koskenalantie campus and the unit of Social Work in the Frami campus area, both located in Seinäjoki.
 The School of Technology offers education in Seinäjoki, mainly in the premises of the Frami campus area.

 Application procedure:
10月15日前，学校提名
Applicant is required to select course(s) or programme relevant to his/her major study. Each exchange student follows one exchange programme whilst at SeAMK.
Programme info: Exchange Programmes | SeAMK
After the nomination the students will receive an email with their SoleMove user account details (IT system which SeAMK is using for mobility applications) and application process instructions on how to make their application. Applications are not accepted by email.
The application deadline is 15 November 2022. In SoleMove system, the required attachments are a photo, transcript of records and learning agreement.
In December, all accepted students will receive an acceptance letter, together with detailed instructions about orientation, arrival and how to apply for student housing etc.
