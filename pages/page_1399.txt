报告题目: Gap probability of the higher order Airy processes and Painleve II hierarchy
报告人：徐帅侠 （中山大学）
报告时间：2022年12月2日9:00-  10:00
报告地点： 腾讯会议647 908 047
报告内容：
 In this talk, we will study the one-parameter family of determinants of the higher order generalization of the Airy kernel. This family of determinants represents a new universal class of distributions, which are higher order analogues of the classical Tracy-Widom distribution.  The determinants can be expressed  in terms of integrals of  solutions of the Painlev\'{e}  II hierarchy. Using the Riemann-Hilbert method, we derive the large gap asymptotics of the determinants up to and including the explicit computation of the constant term with the parameter in (0,1). The singular type asymptotics of the determinants and the associated solutions of the Painlev\'{e} II hierarchy  are also obtained when the parameter is bigger than 1. This talk is based on joint work with Yifan Hao, Jun Xia, Lun Zhang and Yuqiu Zhao.
