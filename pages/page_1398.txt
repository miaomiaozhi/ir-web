报告题目: Weak limit of normalized measures for Askey-Wilson polynomials and other q-polynomials
报告人：汪翔升 （路易斯安那大学拉法叶分校）
报告时间：2022年12月2日  10:00-11:00
报告地点：腾讯会议：647 908 047
报告内容：
We consider the weak limit of the normalized measures for the Askey-Wilson polynomials and some other q-orthogonal polynomials when the parameter q approaches -1 from the right. We use two different methods to prove that the limit is a discrete measure on two mass points that are symmetric about the origin. The weights on these two points are, however, not always the same. We also calculate the weak limit of q-ultraspherical measure when q approaches a complex root of unity.
